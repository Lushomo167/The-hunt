About The Hunt

What is "The Hunt?"

The hunt is a service platform developed in 2019 by lushomo167. It has one goal only; and that is to make sure its user are provided with the best available job opportunities the internet has to offer. After all nobody wants to feel like they wasted four years of their lives for a piece of paper.

The Process

How do you know when we've Got Something For You

Everytime we find vacancies for job opportunities on the web, an email is sent directly to your mailbox where you may view it and respond. Not happy with what you get the first time? No worries. Threads of ads will be sent to you atleast on a daily basis (it all depends on the job), these thread will only be stopped once you have something that is to your satisfication. The Hunt also makes sure to send a copy of that thread to any other email addresses provided by you
